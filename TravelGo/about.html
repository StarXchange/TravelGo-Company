About Travel Go Company

Travel Go Vacations is the leisure travel division of Travel Go Group, a global network of independent travel advisors who specialize in delivering highly personalized travel solutions and expertise to leisure and business travelers.

Travel Go is today a global host travel agency operating in Africa, North America, the UK and Australia supporting a network of more than 750 independent travel advisors.Travel Go is part of the CTM Group of travel businesses.
